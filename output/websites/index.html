Our Story

Woven in tradition,
engineered for today

Filum began in a single weaving shed in 1987 with twelve looms and one conviction: that fabric quality is non-negotiable. Three generations later, we operate 240 looms across two facilities and supply some of the most exacting clients in fashion and interior design.

We don't use the word "premium" loosely. Every metre that leaves our floor has passed colour-fastness, tensile strength, and pilling resistance tests at our in-house laboratory. If it doesn't meet specification, it doesn't ship.

What We Do

  • 01

    Custom Fabric Production

    Woven, knitted, and non-woven textiles produced to your exact specification. Minimum runs from 50m. Delivered to spec or reproduced free of charge.

How It Works

From brief
to bolt

A clear, documented process from your first email to the finished roll leaving our floor.

  1. 01

    Brief

    Send us your specification — weight, weave, colour reference, certifications required. One page is enough.

  2. 02

    Sample

    We produce a lab dip and woven sample within 5–7 days. Approve or iterate — no limit.

  3. 03

    Confirm

    Once the sample is signed off, we lock in lead time and issue a production order acknowledgement.

  4. 04

    Produce

    Weaving and finishing under one roof. You can visit at any point during production.

  5. 05

    Deliver

    Certificate of conformity, packing list, and full test report ship with every order.
